Calculate three phase Px envelopes at a given injection temperature.

Given an injection temperature t_inj and a base PT envelope pt_env_3, finds all the points on the PT envelope near t_inj, based on an absolute tolerance t_tol. These points are used as initialization for calculation of Px envelopes.
